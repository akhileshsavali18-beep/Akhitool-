Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this age calculator?

The calculator is 100% accurate. It takes into account leap years and varying month lengths to calculate your exact age down to the day using your local time zone.

Is my date of birth saved anywhere?

No. All calculations are performed directly within your web browser using JavaScript. No data is sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

How do you calculate total weeks and days?

We calculate the exact number of days between your date of birth and today's date. We then divide that total by 7 to determine the total number of full weeks.
