How to create the perfect business name

Finding the right name for your new business or startup is one of the most crucial early decisions you will make. A great brand name should be memorable, easy to pronounce, and representative of your brand's core values.

Our free AI-powered Business Name Generator helps you brainstorm by taking your seed keywords and combining them with industry-specific terminology, popular brand suffixes (like -ify or -ly), and powerful prefixes to create unique, available name ideas.

Tips for choosing a name:

  • Keep it short: 2 syllables are ideal (e.g., Apple, Google, Twitter).
  • Make it pronounceable: If people can't say it easily, they won't talk about it.
  • Check domain availability: Once you find a name you love, immediately check if the .com or .io domain is available.
  • Ensure it scales: Don't pick a name so specific that it prevents your business from expanding into new product lines later.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the generated business names free to use?

Yes! All names generated by our tool are 100% free for you to use. However, we highly recommend checking local trademark databases in your country to ensure the name hasn't already been registered by another company in your industry.

How does the name generation algorithm work?

The algorithm uses a proprietary mix of linguistic combination techniques. It analyzes your root keywords, applies modern branding trends (like adding specific suffixes or dropping vowels), and merges them with industry-specific lexicons to produce highly brandable outputs.

Does this tool check for domain availability?

Currently, the tool focuses purely on creative name generation and does not perform live domain lookups in order to keep the tool lightning fast and privacy-focused. You will need to check domain availability through a registrar once you select a name.
