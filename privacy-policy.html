Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Welcome to AkhiTool. Your privacy is critically important to us. We have built our tools from the ground up with data security and user privacy in mind. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We believe the best way to protect your privacy is to not collect your data in the first place. Therefore:

  • No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or log in to use our tools.
  • No Tracking of Content: We do not monitor, view, or log the contents of the files, images, or texts you process using our tools.

2. File Processing & Storage

Our tools operate using one of two methods, both designed to protect your privacy:

  • Client-Side Processing: The vast majority of our tools (such as the Image Compressor, Calculators, etc.) run entirely locally within your web browser. Your files never leave your device.
  • Server-Side Processing: For certain complex tools that require server-side power, files are uploaded securely over HTTPS. These files are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within 15 minutes after processing is complete. We keep absolutely no backups of your uploaded files.

3. Log Files & Analytics

Like most websites, we may collect non-personally identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. Our purpose in collecting this non-personally identifying information is to better understand how visitors use the website and to ensure the servers are running smoothly.

4. Cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance your experience (such as remembering your dark mode preference). You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some minor interface features may not function properly without them.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services (such as Google Analytics) to track general website traffic and usage patterns. These services have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:
