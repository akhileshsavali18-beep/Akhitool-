How does the GST Calculator work?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an indirect tax used on the supply of goods and services. Our free tool allows you to perform two essential calculations:

  • Add GST: Used when you have a base price (exclusive of tax) and need to calculate the final price to charge your customers. The calculator adds the applicable GST percentage to the base amount.
  • Remove GST: Used when you have the final invoice amount (inclusive of tax) and need to work backwards to find the original base price and the exact amount of tax that was charged.

This tool works globally and is perfect for business owners, freelancers, and consumers who need quick, accurate tax calculations for accounting or invoicing purposes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the formula to Add GST?

To add GST to a base amount, multiply the base amount by the GST rate, and divide by 100. This gives you the GST Amount. Add this GST Amount to the original base price to get the Total Inclusive Price.

What is the formula to Remove GST?

To remove GST from a total inclusive price, divide the total amount by (1 + (GST Rate / 100)). This gives you the original Base Price. Subtract the Base Price from the Total Amount to find the exact GST Amount.

Is this data saved on your servers?

No. All calculations are performed instantly in your browser using JavaScript. No financial data is ever sent to or stored on our servers.
