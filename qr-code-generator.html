What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that can store a variety of data types, such as website URLs, plain text, email addresses, and phone numbers. Because they can be easily scanned using a smartphone camera, they seamlessly connect the physical world to digital experiences.

Our free online QR code generator makes it incredibly simple to create customized codes for your business or personal use. All processing happens entirely within your browser, ensuring that your data remains completely private and secure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do these QR codes expire?

No, the QR codes generated by our tool are static and will never expire. As long as the destination URL or text remains valid, the QR code will continue to work indefinitely.

Are the generated QR codes free for commercial use?

Yes! You can use the QR codes you generate here for any purpose, including commercial marketing materials, product packaging, menus, and business cards without any licensing fees.

Why is my QR code not scanning?

Scanning issues usually occur due to a lack of contrast between the foreground and background colors. Always ensure your foreground color is significantly darker than your background color. (e.g., Black on White is optimal).
