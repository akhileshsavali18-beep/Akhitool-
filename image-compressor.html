Why use our Image Compressor?

Large image files can slow down your website and consume unnecessary storage. Our free online image compressor uses smart lossy compression techniques to reduce file sizes by up to 80% while keeping visual quality intact.

All processing is handled directly in your browser. This means your private files are never uploaded to our servers, guaranteeing 100% data privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes. Our image compressor works entirely locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
