How to Use the Percentage Calculator

Percentages are a mathematical way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100 (per cent, meaning "per hundred"). Our free percentage calculator is designed to solve the three most common percentage problems encountered in business, math, and daily life:

  • Finding a percentage of a number: Useful for calculating tips, taxes, or discounts. Example: "What is 15% of $120?"
  • Finding out what percentage one number is of another: Ideal for test scores or performance tracking. Example: "45 is what percent of 60?"
  • Calculating percentage change: Perfect for determining price increases, stock market changes, or weight loss. Example: "What is the percentage change from 50 to 65?"

All calculations are done instantly in your browser, maintaining your privacy and ensuring lightning-fast results.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the formula to find a percentage of a number?

To find X% of Y, you convert the percentage to a decimal by dividing it by 100, and then multiply it by Y. The formula is: (X / 100) × Y.

How do I calculate percentage increase or decrease?

To calculate the percentage change between two numbers, subtract the old number from the new number. Then, divide the result by the absolute value of the old number. Finally, multiply by 100. Formula: ((New - Old) / |Old|) × 100.

Can percentages be greater than 100%?

Yes. A percentage greater than 100% simply means the amount is larger than the original whole. For example, a 150% increase means the new value is 2.5 times the original value.
